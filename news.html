News

18 Nov 2005   Many new features have been added to the tool. You may now enter a username, which will be associated with any newly created annotations. You may also download the annotations directly without playing the randomization game. Simply click on the "Downloads" link at the top of the page for more information. We have also added searching and browsing capabilities. To see these features, please go to the introduction page.

24 Aug 2005   Added a link to the "Matlab Toolbox" on the main page. Click on this link to directly access the toolbox which is used for browsing and searching the image set and annotations.

20 Jul 2005   Added automated download capability. Click on the "Get Database" link on the main page for more information on how to obtain the database. Also added the news page (what you are reading now).

Bug Fixes

24 Aug 2005   When finishing the polygon, before entering a name, if you accidentally click on a vertex that is not at the end/beginning of the polygon, you get stuck, i.e. cannot enter a name anymore, cannot delete the segmentation and cannot even get a new image. Thanks to Maryam Mahdaviani of University of British Columbia for pointing this out!
